# Inventory System

## Overview

The inventory stores all items collected by the player throughout the game.

Items are organized into categories to make inventory management easier snd the player can sell items to get gold or use them to upgrade equipmenet.

---

# Item Categories

## Weapons

Weapons increase the player's combat power.

Examples:
- Wooden Sword
- Iron Sword
- Gold Sword
- Diamond Sword
- Knights Sword

Possible Bonuses:
- Attack
- Magic Damage
- Passive Skills

---

## Armor

Armor improves survivability and defense.

Examples:
- Cloth Armor
- Iron Armor
- Gold Armor
- Diamond Armor
- Knights Armor

Possible Bonuses:
- Defense
- Magic Defense
- Resistance

---

## Helmet

Helmet also improves survivability and defense.

Examples:
- Cloth Helmet
- Iron Helmet
- Gold Helmet
- Diamond Helmey
- Knights Helmet

Possible Bonuses:
- Defense
- Magic Defense
- Resistance

---

## Potions

Consumable items that provide immediate effects.

Examples:
- Health Potion with different rarity
- Mana Potion with different rarity

Effects:
- Restore 30% HP
- Restore 50% HP
- Restore 75% HP
- Restore 30% MP
- Restore 50% MP
- Restore 75% MP

---

## Quest Items

Items required for story progression.

Properties:
- Cannot be sold
- Cannot be dropped
- Used only for quests

Examples:
- Ancient Relic
- The knight's mythic sword, armor, and helmet 
 (because the player will need it to defeat the dragon.)

---

## Random Items

Common items obtained from enemies.

Properties:
- Can be sold or used to level up equipment
- No direct story importance

Examples:

From Goblins:
- Goblin Ear
- Broken Dagger

From Bandits:
- Bandit Badge
- Torn Map

From Mountain Enemies:
- Golem Core
- Frost Crystal
- Beast Fang

From Dragon Region:
- Dark Crystal
- Mage Robe Fragment

---

# Inventory Features

The player can:

- View all items
- Sort items by category
- Equip weapons and armor
- Use potions
- View quest items

---

# Item Rarity System

Each weapon and armor piece has a rarity level.

Rarities:

- Common
- Rare
- Epic
- Legendary
- Mythic

Example:

Wooden Sword
Rarity: Common

Iron Sword
Rarity: Rare

Gold Sword
Rarity: Epic

Diamond Sword
Rarity: Legendery

Knights Sword
Rarity: Mythic

---

# Upgrade System

Weapons and armor can be upgraded.

Upgrade Levels:

- +1
- +2
- +3
- +4
- +5

Example:

Rare Sword +3

Benefits:
- Increased stats
- Higher damage and defense
- Improved item value
