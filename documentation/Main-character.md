# Main Character

## Overview

The player controls a single hero throughout the entire game.

The hero starts as an inexperienced adventurer and grows stronger by completing quests, defeating enemies, and progressing through the story.

---

## Starting Stats

Level: 1

HP: 100
MP: 50

Attack: 10
Defense: 5

Magic: 5
Magic Defense: 3

Speed: 5

Gold: 0
EXP: 0

---

## Equipment Slots

The player can equip:

- Weapon
- Helmet
- Armor

---

## Character Progression

The player gains:

- EXP from quests + Battels
- Gold from quests + Enemies
- Items from quests + Enemies + Market

Leveling up increases selected stats.

---
