## AI resolution rules

```text
1. At start of the enemy's own action, reduce cooldowns.
2. Evaluate Group AI when at least one other friendly is alive; otherwise evaluate Solo AI.
3. Read priorities from top to bottom.
4. Skip a rule when its condition, target, cooldown, status, or buff validity fails.
5. Use the first valid rule.
6. If no rule is valid, use Basic Attack against the Player.
7. End the action and resolve durations on the affected unit's own actions.
```

|Constraint|Rule|
|---|---|
|Targeting|All hostile enemy actions target the one Player unless a skill targets Self, Friendly, All Friendlies, or No Target.|
|Status duplicate|Do not reapply a status/buff that has at least 1 own action remaining.|
|Summons|Only Boss patterns may summon. Total living enemy count remains at most 3.|
|Magic-focus test|`Magic Power > Physical Attack`.|
|Physical-focus test|`Physical Attack >= Magic Power`.|
|Boss modifiers|Ancient Dragon Boss Magic `+10%` damage; Dragon King Boss Magic `+25%` damage and `+10%` status chance.|
|Source behavior|Existing source patterns are retained verbatim where the enemy name matches or has a canonical alias.|
