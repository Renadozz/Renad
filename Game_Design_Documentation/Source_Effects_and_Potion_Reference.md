# Potion Crafting and Ingredient Loot

## Crafting Rules

| Potion Tier      | How to Obtain                                                    |
| ---------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Basic Potions    | Craftable with common materials; also available in shops         |
| Improved Potions | Craftable with uncommon materials; limited shop availability     |
| Strong Potions   | Craftable with rare materials from elite enemies or mini bosses  |
| Endgame Potions  | Craftable with boss materials or found as rare boss rewards      |
| Unique Potions   | Boss rewards, quests, treasure chests, or special merchants only |

> Empty Bottles and Empty Oil Flasks can be bought from alchemists, general merchants, or potion shops.
