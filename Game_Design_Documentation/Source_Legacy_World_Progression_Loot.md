# World Progression, Loot, Smithing and Enchanting

## Core Rules

| Rule                   | Description                                                                                                                                                              |
| ---------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Elemental Regions      | There are six elemental regions: Earth, Air, Water, Fire, Darkness, and Holy.                                                                                            |
| Final Region           | Dragon King's Dominion is the seventh and final region.                                                                                                                  |
| Parallel Progression   | All six elemental regions can be entered from the beginning. Their outer areas are similarly difficult.                                                                  |
| Soft Progression Walls | Each region becomes much harder in its deeper areas. Players are expected to switch regions, farm materials, upgrade gear, and return later.                             |
| Maximum Enemy Count    | A combat encounter can contain a maximum of 3 enemies.                                                                                                                   |
| Enemy Reuse            | Some weaker enemies can appear in later regions to create easier encounters and give the player material farming options.                                                |
| Dual Elements          | Enemies can have multiple elements only when the combination is thematic and not contradictory. Holy and Darkness are never combined. Fire and Water are never combined. |
| Potion Drops           | Craftable potions do not normally drop from enemies. Enemies drop their crafting materials instead.                                                                      |
| Boss Farming           | All regional bosses and the Ancient Dragon can be fought again after their first defeat. Their materials remain farmable before the final boss.                          |
| Final Boss Loot        | The Dragon King only drops a quest item. He drops no crafting materials, equipment, Gold, or potions.                                                                    |

# Region Structure

## Region Progression

| Tier   | Player Goal                                                       | Available Areas                                            |
| ------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| Tier 1 | Learn combat, craft basic potions, get Common gear                | Outer areas of all six elemental regions                   |
| Tier 2 | Collect uncommon materials, improve gear, unlock stronger potions | Middle areas of all six elemental regions                  |
| Tier 3 | Fight elites and mini bosses, gain rare materials                 | Deep areas of all six elemental regions                    |
| Tier 4 | Defeat regional bosses and collect Elemental Sigils               | Boss areas of all six elemental regions                    |
| Tier 5 | Enter Dragon King's Dominion and farm endgame materials           | Dragon Valley, Dragon Cave, Obsidian Citadel, Ancient Nest |
| Tier 6 | Defeat the Dragon King and finish the main quest                  | Dragon King's Lair                                         |

# Smith

Weapon upgrade level and weapon grade are separate.

Examples:

* Common Sword +3
* Rare Bow +1
* Legendary Staff +5
* Mythic Dagger +0

> A failed upgrade never destroys or downgrades the weapon.
> Gold and materials are consumed on failure.

| Upgrade | Success Chance | Required Materials                                                  | Gold Cost | Material Sources                                         |

# Enchanter

The Enchanter increases the weapon grade.

`Common → Uncommon → Rare → Epic → Legendary → Mythic`

> A failed enchantment never destroys, downgrades, or changes the weapon.
> Gold and enchantment materials are consumed on failure.

| Grade Upgrade      | Success Chance | Required Materials                                        | Gold Cost | Material Sources                                           |
| ------------------ | -------------: | --------------------------------------------------------- | --------: | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| Common → Uncommon  |            80% | 2x Enchantment Dust                                       |  100 Gold | Goblin Shaman, Forest Witch, Goblin Mage                   |
| Uncommon → Rare    |            60% | 2x Enchantment Fragment                                   |  300 Gold | Sand Wraith, Desert Shaman, Mummy, Ruin Guardian           |
| Rare → Epic        |            40% | 2x Enchantment Crystal                                    |  700 Gold | Mine Wraith, Crystal Golem, Frost Mage, Frost Titan        |
| Epic → Legendary   |            20% | 2x Dragon Rune + 1x Ancient Dragon Scale                  | 1500 Gold | Dragon Priest, Dragon Warlock, Bone Dragon, Ancient Dragon |
| Legendary → Mythic |             8% | 3x Dragon Rune + 1x Mythic Ember + 1x Ancient Dragon Fang | 3000 Gold | Ancient Dragon, Dragon Priest, Dragon Warlock, Bone Dragon |

# Encounter Rules

| Rule                | Description                                                                                           |
| ------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Maximum Enemies     | Maximum 3 enemies per encounter.                                                                      |
| Normal Encounter    | 1 Elite, or 2–3 Normal enemies.                                                                       |
| Hard Encounter      | 1 Elite with 1–2 Normal enemies.                                                                      |
| Mini Boss Encounter | 1 Mini Boss alone, or 1 Mini Boss with 1 Normal enemy.                                                |
| Boss Encounter      | Boss alone or Boss with up to 2 scripted adds.                                                        |
| Solo Challenge      | Every enemy has at least one damaging action and one defensive, control, or buff action.              |
| Support Enemies     | If no ally needs support, they use offensive magic or self-buffs instead of doing nothing.            |
| Reuse Rule          | Earlier enemies can appear in later outer areas at lower levels or in mixed encounters.               |
| Elemental Synergy   | Enemy groups should combine control, defense, and damage rather than using three pure damage enemies. |
