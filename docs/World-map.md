# World Map & Chapter Progression

## Overview

The game world is divided into four main regions. Each region contains enemies, quests, NPCs, and boss encounters. The player progresses through 10 chapters while unlocking new areas and advancing the main story.

---

# World Regions

## Town

The central location of the game.

### Available Services
- Guild
- Market
- Inn (to restore HP + MP)
- Save Point

### Purpose
- Accept quests
- Buy items
- Restore HP and MP
- Manage inventory
- Access unlocked regions

---

## Forest Region

A dangerous forest filled with goblins.

### Areas
- Goblin Forest
- Deep Woods

### Main Enemies
- Goblin dagger
- Goblin Archer
- Goblin Mage
- Goblin King
---

## Desert Region

A harsh desert containing ruins and bandit hideouts.

### Areas
- Bandit Camp
- Ancient Ruins

### Main Enemies
- Bandit
- Bandit Archer
- Bandit leader
- Sand monsters

---

## Mountain Region

A cold mountain range inhabited by powerful monsters.

### Areas
- Mountain Pass
- Frozen Peak

### Main Enemies
- Stone Golem
- Frost Mage
- Mountain Beast

---

## Dragon Region

The final region of the game.

### Areas
- Dragon Valley
- Dragon Cave
- Dragon King's Lair

### Main Enemies
- Dragon Servant/Evil mages
- Dragon Knight
- Ancient Dragon

# Chapter Progression

## Chapter 1 – Goblin Forest

### Location
Forest Region

### Objectives
- Meet the NPC who was captured by goblins
- Learn the game mechanics
- Defeat Goblins and save the NPC
- Go to the town and join the guild

### Enemy
- Goblin dagger
- Goblin Archer
- Goblin Mage

---

## Chapter 2 – Deep Woods

### Location
Forest Region

### Objectives
- Investigate missing travelers
- Explore deeper forest areas

### Boss
- Goblin King

---

## Chapter 3 – Bandit Camp

### Location
Desert Region

### Objectives

- Defeat bandits

- Learn about the dragon servants and the human sacrifices

### Boss

- Bandit
