# Story Script

## Prologue

For centuries, the kingdom lived in peace.
Recently, monsters have begun appearing across the land. Travelers disappear, villages are attacked, and strange rumors spread between towns.
Many believe that an ancient evil has awakened.
The player arrives in a small town searching for adventure, unaware that they will soon face the greatest threat the world has ever known.

---

# Chapter 1 - Goblin Forest

The Guild master asks the hero to investigate attacks near the Goblin Forest.

Objectives:
- Join the Guild
- Learn the basics of combat
- Defeat Goblins

Ending:
The hero discovers that the goblins were forced from their homes by a mysterious power.

---

# Chapter 2 - Deep Woods

Travelers have disappeared in the deeper parts of the forest.

Objectives:
- Search for missing travelers
- Explore the Deep Woods

Ending:
The hero finds evidence of a larger threat spreading across the regions.

---

# Chapter 3 - Bandit Camp

Bandits have begun attacking trade routes.

Objectives:
- Locate the Bandit Camp
- Rescue captured villagers

Ending:
The Bandit Captain reveals that someone has been paying the bandits to create chaos.

---

# Chapter 4 - Ancient Ruins

The hero follows clues leading to ancient ruins in the desert.

Objectives:
- Explore the ruins
- Recover an ancient relic

Ending:
The relic reveals information about the ancient Dragon's return.

---

# Chapter 5 - Mountain Pass

The hero travels to the mountains searching for answers.

Objectives:
- Reach the Mountain Pass
- Defeat mountain creatures

Ending:
The path to the frozen peak is opened.

---

# Chapter 6 - Frozen Peak

Ancient records are hidden at the top of the mountain.

Objectives:
- Climb the Frozen Peak
- Recover lost knowledge

Ending:
The hero learns that the ancient Dragon servants are gathering power in the east.

---

# Chapter 7 - Dragon Valley

The hero enters the Dragon Region.

Objectives:
- Investigate Dragon Valley
- Defeat Dragon Servants

Ending:
The hero discovers the entrance to the Dragon Cave.

---

# Chapter 8 - Dragon Cave

The hero explores the cave beneath the valley.

Objectives:
- Survive the cave
- Defeat powerful mages

Ending:
The location of the ancient Dragon's lair is revealed.

---

# Chapter 9 - Dragon Territory

The hero prepares for the final battle.

Objectives:
- Prepare for the final battle
- Defeat the Kinghts Captain and obtain a mythic armor

Ending:
The path to the ancient Dragon's Lair is opened.

---

# Chapter 10 - ancient Dragon's Lair

The hero enters the final dungeon.

Objectives:
- Reach the dragon liar
- Defeat the ancient dragon

Ending:
The Dragon is defeated.
Peace returns to the world.
The hero becomes a legendary adventurer and protector of the kingdom.
