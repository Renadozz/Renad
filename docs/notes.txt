Story Progression:
Instead of 10 chapters, i will use only 3 chapters:
Chapter 1 – Goblin Forest
Defeat Goblins and complete basic quests.

Chapter 2 – Bandit Camp
Fight stronger enemies and gain more experience.

Chapter 3 – Dragon Cave
Defeat the Dragon Boss and finish the game.

Quests:
Only using Main Quests for the first version.

Defeat the Dragon

Battle System:
Simple turn-based combat.
-Player Actions
1. Attack
2. Skill
3. Use Potion

-Battle Flow
1. Player attacks first.
2. Enemy attacks.
3. Repeat until one side reaches 0 HP.
